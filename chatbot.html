AI Portfolio Assistant — End-to-End Architecture

A full-stack, production-grade Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot designed to engage users and provide dynamic responses based on my professional background.

Problem & Solution

The Problem

Static portfolios often fail to engage visitors effectively. Users may have specific questions about a developer's experience or skills that aren't immediately obvious from a list of bullet points.

The Solution

An AI-powered assistant that uses RAG to answer questions dynamically. By retrieving context from my specific data, it provides accurate, personalized information 24/7.
